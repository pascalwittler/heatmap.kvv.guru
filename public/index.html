About this project

The map provides a quick overview of the traffic density of tram and bus routes in the city of Karlsruhe. The data comes from a GTFS archive provided as open data by the Karlsruher Verkehrsverbund (KVV). Each of the points is a stop positioned by longitude and latitude. The more trams and buses depart from a stop according to the timetable, the larger the dot on the map.
